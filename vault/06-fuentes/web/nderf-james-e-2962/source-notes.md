# Notas de extracción — James E NDE 2962

Fuente: [James E NDE 2962](http://www.nderf.org/Experiences/1james_e_nde.html) / NDERF.

> Nota: esto no es una transcripción. Es una capa de notas/extractos para trazabilidad editorial desde una fuente web textual.

## Condición médica relatada

> "Advanced Hodgkin's lymphoma cancer. I was dying of cancer, the doctors after three years of treatments, had put me in a cushy room to die. I had fell into a coma after a second surgery in which they tried to physically remove tumors from inside me, the cancer has spread all throughout my body. The coma lasted five days. It was during this time that I had the experience."

Importancia: punto de partida extremo: cáncer avanzado, tratamientos prolongados, cirugía, coma de cinco días y expectativa de muerte.

## Entrada en la luz / unidad

> "Then, POOF, I'm in the light. It's golden, it's beautiful, the pain from the three years of my life gone... I was one with everything that ever existed, or ever will be."

Importancia: experiencia de luz, desaparición del dolor y unidad/interconexión.

## Mensaje de retorno

> "Then it spoke to me... It simply said, 'You have to go back,' and it was me telling myself I have to go back; but yet - I am all, so who was talking to whom???"

Importancia: mensaje de retorno claro, asociado a `no-es-tu-momento`, con comunicación no verbal/identidad expandida.

## Curación física relatada

> "After it told me I had to come back, that was it. I woke up in my bed, my mother lying next to me, five days had passed, the cancer that was eating me alive, that the doctors had said they could not cure, was completely healed. They tested me, found I was completely void of cancer, and sent me home."

Importancia: núcleo de `curacion-extrema-post-ecm`: retorno tras coma y pruebas que, según el relato, no encontraron cáncer.

## Temporalidad y conocimiento

> "Everything seemed to be happening all at once..."

> "Everything was known in that instant I entered the light."

Importancia: simultaneidad y conocimiento directo.

## Percepción no física

> "In the darkness, and in the light my perception was 360 degrees. I saw all around me at all times. I did not have 'binocular' vision."

> "Without a body, there is no brain to be telepathic with; so it was more like, without ears how can you hear? Without eyes, how can you see?"

Importancia: percepción sin órganos físicos; útil para `identidad-mas-alla-del-cuerpo` y quizá para matizar percepción no física, aunque no hay escena física verificable como en Kevin.
